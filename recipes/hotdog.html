Hot Dog Ramen

Regular | Corn Beef | Home

Description

When you want to add a little protein to your simple ramen dish, this is the answer. Simple and quick for everyone who doesn't have the time or energy to do anything more fancy, but who is sick of plain ramen.

Ingredients

  • 1 Maruchan Chicken Ramen Packet
  • 3 cups Water
  • 1 tbsp Jamaican Grace Hot Sauce
  • One pack of uncooked chicken hotdogs
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Boil between two and four chicken hot dogs. (The number of hot dogs is up to the chef's discretion)
  2. When hot dogs are cooked through, remove from water and slice into long strips.
  3. Sear the long hot dog strips in a pan, and set aside.
  4. Add water and the noodles from the Maruchan packet into a pot and boil on medium-high heat until noodles are firm, but a little springy.
  5. Drain all of the water and plate noodles into a bowl.
  6. Add ketchup to noodles to taste, and mix until noodles are adequately covered in ketchup.
  7. Top with hot sauce to taste.
  8. Add sliced hot dogs on top of noodles and eat.
